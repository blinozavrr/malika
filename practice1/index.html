Planets of the Solar System

The Solar System is made up of eight planets, which orbit the Sun. These planets are:

  1. Mercury
  2. Venus
  3. Earth
  4. Mars
  5. Jupiter
  6. Saturn
  7. Uranus
  8. Neptune

Interesting Facts about the Solar System

"The Sun contains 99.86% of the mass in the Solar System."

Did you know that the Solar System is more than 4.5 billion years old? Also, the distance between the Earth and the Sun is about 93 million miles (150 million kilometers).
